Erik Jhones Freitas do Nascimento

Since may 2023, I am an artificial inteligence developer, focusing on Natural Language Processing (NLP), at IFCE's Polo IFCE. I am interested in Bayesian inference, deep learning, and graph neural network. The common thread here is to strive for greener (compute-efficient) AI.

Follow me on twitter @erik_jfn for somewhat regular updates and for occasional bad jokes. You can also contact me through erikjhonesf@gmail.com

I am committed to foster diversity and encourage folks from different backgrounds (e.g., nationality, sex, gender, and religion) to contact me.
